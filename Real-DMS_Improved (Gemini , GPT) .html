Create your local identity

What is created?

A local cryptographic identity is generated inside your browser and saved to IndexedDB on this device. Private keys never leave the device unless you explicitly export them.

• Username uniqueness is enforced locally among saved contacts.
• Direct messaging needs a token exchange because there is no permanent backend.
• Chat history is saved locally and remains readable offline.
